As the human population increases exponentially and because the illegal wildlife trade is so profitable; the demand for wildlife animals and their parts has increased phenomenally. Poaching includes Illegal hunting, capturing, and killing of wildlife animals.

How does poaching affect the Ecosystem?

Everything we interact with, living or nonliving, is attached to an ecosystem one way or another; and so is poaching. From the smallest possible microorganism to the largest organism, natural ecosystems are greatly endangered by poaching.
